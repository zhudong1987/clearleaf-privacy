Clearleaf Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Data Not Collected

Clearleaf ("the app") is designed to be private by default. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your personal information. This policy explains what that means.

1. Information we collect

None that leaves your device. Everything you enter in Clearleaf — your start date, check-ins, streaks, consumption methods, spending, dream journal entries, and settings — is stored only locally on your iPhone. We have no servers and no account system, so this information is never sent to us or to anyone else.

2. Analytics and tracking

Clearleaf contains no analytics SDKs, no advertising, and no third-party trackers. We do not profile you and we do not track you across apps or websites.

3. Purchases

Clearleaf offers a one-time, optional in-app purchase to unlock premium features. Payments are processed by Apple (and our purchase provider, RevenueCat) under their own privacy policies. We never see or store your payment details. We only receive an anonymous signal indicating whether the purchase is active, so the app can unlock premium features.

4. Your data and your control

Because your data lives on your device, you are in full control. Deleting the app removes all of its data from your phone. We hold no copy and cannot access it.

5. Children

Clearleaf is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.

6. Medical disclaimer

Clearleaf is a self-help tracking tool and is not medical advice. It does not diagnose, treat, or replace care from a qualified health professional. If you are struggling, please consult a doctor or a local support service.

7. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
